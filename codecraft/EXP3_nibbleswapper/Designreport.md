## DESIGN REPORT

### PROBLEM STATEMENT

Swap upper and lower 4 bits of an 8-bit input.

### USE CASE

Used in data manipulation, encoding, and low-level bit operations.

### DESIGN REQUIREMENTS

* Input: 8-bit data
* Output: swapped 8-bit data

### DESIGN CONSTRAINTS

* Must be combinational
* No delay in swapping

### DESIGN METHODOLOGY & IMPLEMENTATION DETAILS

Bit concatenation is used to rearrange the lower and upper 4 bits of the input.

### FUNCTIONAL SIMULATION METHODOLOGY & TEST CASES

Various inputs tested to verify correct swapping.

### RESULTS & ANALYSIS

All outputs matched expected swapped values.

### CHALLENGES & CONCLUSIONS

Simple and efficient bit manipulation achieved.
